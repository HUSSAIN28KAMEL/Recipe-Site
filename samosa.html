Samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 cup boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Time

45 minutes

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, water, and oil to make dough; let it rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling by saut√©ing cumin seeds, potatoes, peas, and spices.
  3. Roll dough into small circles, fill with potato mixture, and fold into triangular shapes.
  4. Deep-fry samosas until golden brown and crispy.
