Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Time

1.5 hours

Instructions

  1. Cook lasagna sheets as per package instructions.
  2. Cook ground beef in a skillet, add marinara sauce, and simmer.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Layer lasagna sheets, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers, top with Parmesan, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
