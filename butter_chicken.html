Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (boneless, cubed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste

Preparation Time

1 hour

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, garam masala, and ginger-garlic paste for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken in a pan until lightly browned.
  3. In another pan, heat butter, add tomato puree, and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add cream, cooked chicken, and salt; simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.
